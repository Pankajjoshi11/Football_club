WELCOME TO CHURCHILL UNITED FC !

Churchill United FC is a football club located in Churchgate, South Mumbai. Passionate about tactics, player development and making football fun and accessible to everyone.

ABOUT US

Welcome to Churchill United, a football club that embodies the spirit and passion of Mumbai's vibrant sports culture. Established with a vision to elevate the beautiful game within the bustling heart of the city, Churchill United stands as a symbol of unity, determination, and excellence. With a rich tapestry of diverse talents converging on the field, our club strives to not only excel in the sport but also foster a strong sense of community and camaraderie among players and fans alike. As we chase our goals on the pitch, we are equally committed to contributing to the development of football at the grassroots level in Mumbai. Through rigorous training, strategic gameplay, and an unyielding spirit, Mumbai United aims to carve its name into the annals of Indian football while inspiring generations and leaving an indelible mark on the city we proudly call home.

Our Coaches
